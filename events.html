2. Regular Yoga Classes for campus residents:

 

In August 2014, NRCVEE joined Board for Sports Activities (BSA) for starting regular yoga sessions in the morning as well as evening by trained yoga instructors. To ensure seriousness in the participants, these classes are paid with monthly registration. The classes have been running for more than three years now.

 

3. Gandhi Jayanti Celebrations:

 

Since 2012, Dean of Students' office and NRCVEE have bene jointly organising the institute function on October 2. The salient features of the Gandhi Jayanti celebrations in 2016 were as follows:

 

• Bhajans and Sadbhavana Geet by institute community including students, staff and families of staff and faculty.

• Recognition of the work by the cleaning staff of the institute by presenting coupons of Rs 500 each to selected 30 contractual staff. These coupons could be used at the Khadi Gramodyog (KVIC) shop in the IITD campus.

• Fancy dress competition on the theme “Gandhi in Me”

 

4. Organisation of International Yoga Day:

 

On June 21, 2017, the third International Day of the Yoga was organised by IITD. NRCVEE was given the main responsibility for planning the same and it was implemented with the help of Wellness Club, IIT-Delhi. Though it was vacation period at IITD, several events were organised and were well-attended. One month regular Yoga practice sessions were organised in  with the help from a qualified Yoga instructor trained at Morarji Desai National Institute of Yoga. At IIT Delhi the following events were organised: (All the events were bilingual to help all the campus residents to get the benefit of the same)

2. Art of Happiness Workshop by Brahamkumaris
